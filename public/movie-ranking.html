The Best Movies According to Azher

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Revenant

This is my favourite thriller. This movie is based on a novel about real people and real history. .

The Lion King

This is my favourite anime. Lion prince Simba and his father are targeted by his bitter uncle, who wants to ascend the throne himself.

The Everest

Super beautiful film. This is the true story of two different expeditions in 1996 who attempt to scale Everest but encounter massive storms on the descent down the mountain..
